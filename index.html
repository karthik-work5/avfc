About the League

Cricket at its
Finest Level

The AVFC Cricket League is a premier club-level tournament designed to celebrate the spirit of cricket. From powerful openers to crafty spinners, every player gets their moment to shine.

Played in T10 format, the league brings together communities, friendships, and the love of the game onto one legendary stage.
